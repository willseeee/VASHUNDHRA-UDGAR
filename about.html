About Us

Empowering rural communities and restoring natural ecosystems for a sustainable future.

Overview

Vashundhara Udgar Foundation Trust (VUFT) is a premier non-governmental organization committed to transforming rural India. Our integrated approach spans across agriculture, climate action, rural development, and IT education.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To implement sustainable solutions that empower rural communities, restore natural ecosystems, and promote technological literacy.

Our Vision

A self-reliant, resilient, and ecologically balanced rural India.

Founder's Message

Mr. John Doe

"When we started VUFT, our goal was simple: to ensure that the progress of the world does not leave our rural communities and our environment behind. Today, our integrated approach to climate-smart agriculture and IT education is building a bridge to a better future."

Core Values

  • Integrity: Absolute transparency in all we do.
  • Empowerment: Enabling communities to lead their own development.
  • Sustainability: Ensuring long-term ecological balance.
  • Innovation: Leveraging technology for rural solutions.

Our Expertise

  • Agroforestry & Climate Action
  • Women's Livelihood Generation
  • Rural IT & Smart Learning Centers
  • Project Implementation & Monitoring
