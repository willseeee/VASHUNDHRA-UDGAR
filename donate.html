We maintain a strict 92% direct-deployment index. This means 92% of your donated funds go directly into purchasing tree saplings, computers, and water equipment in villages.

80G Tax Exemption Available

Vashundhara Udgar Foundation Trust is registered under Section 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act. All contributions are eligible for a 50% tax deduction. Receipts will be dispatched automatically to your registered email.
