About VUFT →

Vashundhara Udgar Foundation Trust is a leading non-profit organization dedicated to holistic rural development. We integrate climate action, agriculture, and IT education to create sustainable livelihoods.

Our mission is to build a future in which rural communities live in harmony with nature.

Vision Snapshot

To foster resilient, self-reliant rural ecosystems through innovation, education, and ecological restoration.
